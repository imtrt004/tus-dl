About TuS-DL

Learn more about our universal media downloader and how it works

Our Story

TuS-DL was created out of the need for a reliable, easy-to-use solution for downloading media from across the web. We built this tool to help users save content for offline viewing, research, and educational purposes.

Our focus has always been on combining powerful features with a simple, intuitive interface that anyone can use. TuS-DL is continuously updated to support the latest websites and maintain compatibility with evolving web standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TuS-DL free to use?

Yes, TuS-DL is completely free and open-source. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.

Is downloading content legal?

TuS-DL is meant for downloading content for personal use, such as watching offline or archiving your own content. Always respect copyright laws and terms of service for the websites you use.

Why do some downloads require cookies?

Some websites require you to be logged in to access certain content. In these cases, TuS-DL needs your browser cookies to authenticate the download request.

Are my downloads private?

Yes. We don't store any information about what you download, and files are automatically deleted from our servers after you download them.

Can I contribute to TuS-DL?

Absolutely! Visit our GitHub repository to contribute code, report bugs, or suggest new features. We welcome contributions from developers of all experience levels.
